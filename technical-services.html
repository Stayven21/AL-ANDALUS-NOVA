Our Story

Established in 2010, AL ANDALUS TECHNICAL SERVICES L.L.C specializes in designing and building custom-made exhibition stands. With offices in both the UAE, Turkey, Europe and Middle East, we have proudly served clients across the Middle East as well as internationally.

We are a dynamic Dubai-based exhibition stand design and contracting company, offering full turnkey services — from conceptualization to completion. Our mission is to ensure that every client is fully satisfied through exceptionally creative designs, innovative ideas, and high-quality project execution.

Our Vision

To create innovative and functional environments that combine creativity, efficiency, and high-quality execution to ensure complete client satisfaction.

Our Approach

At AL ANDALUS TECHNICAL SERVICES L.L.C, we don't just build stands or decorate office spaces — we create efficient environments that enable you to maximize your space and budget. We work closely with our clients to achieve a seamless integration of concepts, opportunities, design, and process.

As our client, you can rely on us to deliver a complete design and project management package. Our goal is to help you develop a highly innovative, ergonomic environment that enhances functionality while maintaining exceptional quality and professionalism.
